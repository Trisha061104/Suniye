    FAQ's

    Suniye focuses on providing speech and language education to hearing-impaired children.

    Suniye offers specialized education, speech therapy, and audiometry services.

    Suniye is based in New Delhi, India, but serves hearing-impaired children across the country.

    Suniye primarily works with children aged 3 to 18 years.

    Suniye provides affordable services and support to ensure accessibility for families in need.

    Individuals can support Suniye through donations, volunteering, or spreading awareness.

    Yes, Suniye collaborates with NGOs, schools, and health professionals for better service delivery.

    Suniye facilitates hearing aid fittings in collaboration with trusted audiologists.

    Yes, Suniye employs certified educators and therapists trained to work with hearing-impaired children.

    Enrollment can be done by contacting Suniye through the website or visiting their center.

    About Us

    Our aim is to train hearing impaired children in speech and language so that they can maximise their potential and make their own choices in their career and in life.
